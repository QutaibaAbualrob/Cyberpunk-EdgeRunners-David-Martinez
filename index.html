David Martinez
A Night City Legend

David Martinez, born to Gloria Martinez, grew up on the tough streets of Santo Domingo in Night City. Raised by his hardworking mother, David excelled academically and attended Arasaka Academy.

A turning point came when his mother tragically died after being denied critical care. Devastated and fueled by grief, David implanted a powerful military-grade Sandevistan cyberware, setting him on a dangerous path toward becoming an Edgerunner.

Education

David Martinez attended the prestigious Arasaka Academy thanks to his mother’s hard work.
He excelled academically but struggled to fit in due to his lower-class background.
To cope, he began selling illegal XBDs to classmates, leading to conflicts with wealthier students.
His rebellious behavior eventually led the school to suggest he find a different environment.

Experience

Martinez in Night City’s broken system, where corporate control left
public services weak and ineffective. As an edgerunner, he took on dangerous jobs, providing
protection, delivering justice, and performing high-risk tasks. His work filled the gap left by corrupt
authorities. After installing the Sandevistan, he fully immersed himself in this violent, chaotic lifestyle.

Skills

Skilled in both hand-to-hand combat and firearms, David was a formidable fighter. His use of the
Sandevistan cyberware granted him superhuman speed and reflexes, enabling him to outpace and
outthink opponents. As a natural leader, he guided his team through high-risk operations with
precision. Adaptable and resilient, he thrived in the chaotic world of Night City, mastering
the art of survival as an edgerunner.
